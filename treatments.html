Treatments We Offer

Manicure & Pedicure

Maintain healthy looking hands and feet; we offer cuticle and nail shaping, filing and polish application. Be pampered and preened, enjoy therapy for your hands and feet. When you need a bit of pampering our manicures and pedicures are a must.

Facials

Unleash your natural beauty with our extensive range of facial treatments that are designed to soothe skin, smooth fine lines and boost your confidence to help you love the skin you are in. Working with award-winning brands, our facials offer everything from a quick radiance fix to more intensive lasting results. We will cleanse, hydrate and exfoliate your complexion to leave you feeling refreshed and brighter.

Eyerows & Eyelashes

Beauty treats to ensure you leave looking pristine. We offer eyebrow shaping and tinting, eyelash tinting and extensions.

Massages

Massage has many benefits from relaxing tired muscles to improving circulation and reliving tension andstress. We offer a range of massages including sports, holistic and therapeutic aromatherapy massages.
